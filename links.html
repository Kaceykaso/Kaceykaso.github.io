Links

Smashing Magazine is a website for web designers, developers, UI/UX, graphic designers, and anything in between; catering to all skills levels. Articles on theory, tips and tricks, breaking news, new library or API demos, and portfolio or case study showcases are posted from a variety of authors on a regular basis. Anything from how to make your own WordPress theme, to monthly calendar design contests, to dicussions on speed differences between JQuery and CSS3 animations, and even bundling your own Ruby gems.

Smashing has published books on topics such as the Mobile Web, Front-End Design, Digital Adaptation management practices, and new perspectives on Web Design. The site also features industry job boards, as well as workshops to brush up on any forgotten skills you might find on thse job boards.

This site is a daily stop for myself, and many of my coworkers, for years now.

Webdesigner Depot is a website dedicated to all things web design and development; from wireframes and rapid prototyping, to server-side response times and scalable architecture. The site posts regular articles on design case studies, tutorials and demos, and even the latest icon and boilerplate freebie downloads.

This particular site recently went through a complete redesign, and posted an extensive case study on it; detailing every design decision and troubleshooting point their team went through. An interesting read for designers at any skill level.
